Why SIGCE


  • Start-up Incubation Center: with vision of producing "Job Creators", SIGCE has set up a fully functional Start-up incubation center eco-system
  • Campus: Sprawling new state of the art campus, conveniently located in the heart of Navi Mumbai at Ghansoli, in proximity to Reliance Jio headquarters and Thane-Belapur Industrial Corridor
  • Industry: Academia Collaboration: Dedicated floor for industry sponsored labs and courses
  • Advanced Labs: Robotics, Mechatronics, 3D Printing offering hands on learning training courses
  • Emerging Technologies: Offer certified courses in collaboration with industry expert in AI, Blockchain and Big data and Analytics
  • Placement: 100% placement assistance for qualified students.
  • Faculty-student synergy: In today's information-rich environment, it is this synergy which enables the student to think and perform beyond the boundaries of the regular curriculum
  • Technocrat 3.0: Dynamic, interactive, hands-on, digital learning environment, which aims to train future technocrats with global perspective
